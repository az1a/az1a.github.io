Privacy Policy for Dialogia

Effective date: 2026-06-16

This Privacy Policy describes the processing of personal data in connection with the mobile application Dialogia. The identity and contact details of the controller are set out in Section 12 of this Privacy Policy.

1. Categories of Personal Data

Depending on the manner in which the application is used, the following categories of personal data may be processed:

  • account and authentication data, including anonymous Firebase user identifiers and, where applicable, email address and authentication credentials processed through Firebase Authentication;
  • application settings and preference data, including language selections, onboarding choices, consent status, locale, theme, reminder settings, and region or country code used for consent logic;
  • learning and usage data, including progress, scores, total learned items, saved AI-generated dialogs, saved dictionary phrases, translations, practice counts, and whether a saved phrase came from an AI-generated dialog so that the correct grammar-cache policy can be applied;
  • speech-related data, including microphone input, speech-recognition results made available by the device or platform service, and speech-recognition quality reports submitted by the user;
  • AI feature input and output data, including prompts for dialog generation, sentences submitted for grammar explanation, and generated responses;
  • subscription and entitlement data, including purchase status and subscription status processed through RevenueCat and the relevant app store platform;
  • analytics data, where analytics is enabled in accordance with applicable law, such as app interaction events, onboarding completion, selected learning and native languages, motivation choice, consent status, region or country code used for consent logic, subscription event status, product identifier where applicable, and device or app identifiers; and
  • technical and security data, including IP address, request metadata, timestamps, and authentication tokens processed in connection with backend requests.

2. Purposes of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • provision and operation of the application;
  • user authentication, account administration, and synchronization of eligible data;
  • provision of AI-assisted features;
  • management of subscriptions and premium entitlements;
  • operation of speech-practice functionality;
  • storage of user preferences and reminders;
  • analytics and service improvement, where permitted;
  • security, abuse prevention, quota enforcement, and service integrity; and
  • compliance with legal obligations.

2A. AI Features and Grammar Cache

Dialogia offers AI-assisted features such as dialog generation and grammar explanations. Users should not submit sensitive personal information in AI prompts, generated-dialog situations, or sentences submitted for grammar explanation.

For built-in app-provided dialog sentences, grammar explanations may be cached without user identifiers using a sentence hash, target language, native language, and generated explanation text. This cache is used to avoid repeated AI requests and may be reused for other users requesting the same built-in sentence and language pair.

Grammar explanations for AI-generated dialogs or user-influenced saved lines are not intentionally stored in the shared grammar cache. Such sentences and generated explanations are still processed as necessary to provide the grammar explanation feature.

2B. No Sale, Ads, or Advertising Tracking

Dialogia does not sell personal data. Dialogia does not display third-party advertising. Dialogia does not intentionally use personal data to track users across apps or websites for third-party advertising purposes.

Some third-party service providers, such as Google Firebase, RevenueCat, OpenAI, Apple, and Google, may process data as described in their own privacy notices and as necessary to provide their services.

3. Legal Bases

Where Regulation (EU) 2016/679 applies, processing is carried out on the following legal bases, as applicable:

  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR, where processing is necessary for the performance of a contract or in order to take steps at the request of the data subject prior to entering into a contract;
  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR, where processing is based on consent, including, where applicable, analytics and certain device permissions;
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, where processing is necessary for the purposes of legitimate interests, in particular security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, quota enforcement, and reliable operation of the service; and
  • Article 6(1)(c) GDPR, where processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation.

Where required by applicable law, analytics remains disabled unless and until the relevant consent has been granted or the application determines that such consent is not required.

4. Recipients and Third-Party Services

Personal data may be processed by the following categories of recipients and service providers, to the extent necessary for the purposes stated above:

  • Google Firebase, including authentication, analytics, and backend infrastructure services;
  • RevenueCat, for subscription and entitlement management;
  • OpenAI, for AI-generated dialogs and grammar explanations;
  • Apple and Google, including the relevant app store and platform services; and
  • speech-recognition services made available by the operating system or platform, where used by the device.

The controller does not receive full payment card details. Payments are processed by the relevant app store platform.

5. Retention

Personal data is retained only for so long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

In particular:

  • data stored solely on the device remains stored until deleted by the user, removed by clearing application data, or removed by uninstallation;
  • account-linked backend data is generally retained for the duration of the account relationship;
  • daily AI and grammar usage records linked to an account are removed when the user deletes the account through the in-app deletion function;
  • speech-recognition quality reports may be retained without account linkage for quality-improvement purposes;
  • shared grammar cache records may be retained without user identifiers until they are no longer needed for app functionality, quality improvement, abuse prevention, or maintenance; and
  • limited technical and operational records may be retained where necessary for security, abuse prevention, reliability, or legal compliance.

6. Account Deletion

The application includes an in-app account deletion function. Upon account deletion, account-linked backend data associated with that account is deleted, including synced progress, synced AI dialogs, consent data stored in the user profile, and account-linked daily AI and grammar usage records, subject to any overriding legal, security, fraud-prevention, or technical retention requirements.

Speech-recognition quality reports submitted for quality-improvement purposes are not linked to the account identifier in the report collection and may therefore not be removable as account-linked data.

Shared grammar cache records for built-in app-provided dialog sentences are not linked to the account identifier and may therefore not be removable as account-linked data.

Account deletion does not cancel active subscriptions. Subscriptions must be cancelled through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Purchase, subscription, and entitlement records may continue to be retained by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat according to their own legal, financial, and operational requirements.

7. Microphone and Speech Features

The application requests access to the microphone solely for the purpose of enabling speech-practice and speech-recognition functionality initiated by the user.

Raw audio recordings are not stored on the controller's own backend as part of ordinary operation of the application.

7A. Children

Dialogia is not directed to children under 13 years of age. If you believe that a child has provided personal data through the application, please contact the controller so that appropriate action can be taken.

8. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data. Where personal data is transmitted to backend services, transmission is performed using modern encrypted transport protocols.

9. Data Subject Rights

Where applicable law provides such rights, data subjects may have the right to request access to personal data, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection to processing, data portability, withdrawal of consent at any time with future effect, and the lodging of a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

10. International Data Transfers

Where personal data is processed outside Germany or the European Economic Area, such processing is carried out subject to the safeguards required by applicable law.

11. Amendments

This Privacy Policy may be amended from time to time. The version published for the application shall be authoritative as of its stated effective date.
