When Contacting Support

To help resolve your request faster, please include:

  • a short description of the issue or question;
  • your device model and operating system version;
  • the LaVerba app version, if available;
  • screenshots or error messages, if relevant; and
  • whether the issue relates to subscriptions, account access, speech practice, AI dialogs, or another feature.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Deleting the app or deleting an account does not cancel an active subscription.
